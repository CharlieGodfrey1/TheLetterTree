About

Explore all of the illuminating designs The Letter Tree has to offer.

Based in Worcestershire, The Letter Tree provides high quality and beautifully unique letter light hire across the midlands area and beyond.

All of our light up letters for hire are handmade by skilled professionals who have more than 25 years' experience in the industry. We can guarantee high quality designs that make an impact at any occasion.

The founder of The Letter Tree, Deborah Godfrey has a background in art and design, as well as working in the wedding industry for almost a decade, means she has a keen eye for trends as well as knowing the importance of making your big occasion look it's very best.

Accompanied by a small team, Deborah and The Letter Tree is devoted to delivering great customer service and a desire to provide everyone with amazing illuminating letters.

Our ever expanding company has two main letter sizes. The first being our range of 4ft light up letters. These are ideal for indoor events that need a finishing touch to the decor or theme. Our second line is made up of our show stopping 6ft light up letters for hire. These giant letters for hire are the main event and ensure you and your guests are wowed at any event.

Our letters are highly popular with celebratory events and parties. Plus, our light up letters hired for weddings always give you and guests amazing photography backgrounds, as well as an individual way to decorate your event like never before.

In addition to our classic love letters for wedding letter hire, we also provide for corporate, commercial and promotional events where the stunning letters can spell out company names, brand titles and popular hashtags.

What's more, The Letter Tree can easily create bespoke designs that fit perfectly with any theme, be that a wedding day, a birthday celebration or promotional and corporate events.

All of our small or large letters for hire can be professionally sprayed your desired colour with a high quality finish. As well as this, we can also incorporate patterns and styles such as stripes, polka dots and pastels.

Not looking for colour? The Letter Tree also deliver a range of wood tones, from deep browns to honey shades that look good indoors or out.

Don't hesitate to take a look at our designs on the gallery page, as well as our Instagram @thelettertree, where we showcase our amazing letters at all event types.
